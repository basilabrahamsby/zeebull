About Serava
One Platform. Every Department. Zero Gaps.

Serava is an end-to-end hotel and resort management system built entirely for Indian hospitality. From the moment a guest books a room to the moment they check out — and every department in between — Serava keeps everything connected, automated, and GST-compliant. Replace scattered spreadsheets and paper registers with a single cloud platform your entire team can rely on.

MODULE 07
Built-In Double-Entry Accounting
Chart of Accounts
  • 42 ledgers across 7 account groups
  • Room, Food, Service, Package Revenue
  • Cash in Hand, Bank (HDFC / SBI)
  • Accounts Receivable (Guest)
  • Inventory Asset (Stock)
  • Property, Plant & Equipment
  • Accounts Payable (Vendor)
  • Advance Deposits (Liability)
  • COGS, Salaries, Utilities, Marketing
Automatic Journal Entries
  • Guest checkout → Revenue + Output GST
  • Cash/Card/UPI → Correct debit ledger
  • Inventory purchase → Stock + Input GST
  • RCM expenses → Self-invoice + payable
  • Discount → Discount Allowed ledger
  • Every entry balanced on creation
  • Manual override always available
Financial Reports
  • Trial Balance (auto-calculated)
  • Profit & Loss Statement
  • Comprehensive Business Report
  • Ledger-wise transaction history
  • Revenue breakdown by module
  • Date-range filtering on all reports
